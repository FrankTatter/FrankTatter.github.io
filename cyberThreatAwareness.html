Cyber Threat Awareness

Year : 2023

Languages : AWS, PHP, JavaScript, HTML, CSS

In school, my teacher was doing an assembly and I wanted to help by creating a fake version of the schools web filtering website to try and trick students into entering their personal information to show them how not all sites on the internet can be trusted.

However when we trialed the site on a class, just displaying their usernames on the screen and showing only them their username and password, this didn't quite get the message across. So I added a feature which would take an embarrassing photo of them, making them realize that it was the site which had taken their data.

First I copied the schools internet log in page which I used to trick the students in to entering their personal information which I would then add to a database and upload to a separate site which the teacher could then display to show the students who got fooled by the site.
